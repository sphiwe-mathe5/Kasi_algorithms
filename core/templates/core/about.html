Executive Summary

Kasi Algorithms Solutions was founded with the belief that technology can be a powerful tool for solving township problems. Townships face many challenges, including access to education, healthcare, efficient public services, business management and economic empowerment. However, there is growing access to smartphones and the internet, presenting a unique opportunity for technology to bridge this gap. Kasi Algorithms Solutions aims at or rather try to provide solutions for such problems.
